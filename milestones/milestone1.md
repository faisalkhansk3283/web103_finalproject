## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?
Our team was able to fork the repository and get everyone access quickly, which let us move on to the planning work without delay. We also collaborated well on brainstorming, going through several app ideas before landing on one that clearly met all the required database relationships and features.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?
The biggest challenge was narrowing down our app idea. We considered several concepts before settling on a budget tracker, since we wanted something that would naturally support both a one-to-many and many-to-many relationship while still being interesting to build and demo.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?
As we move into wireframing and building our entity relationship diagram in Milestone 2, we may need guidance on how to best structure the join table between transactions and categories, especially since we plan to include a unique field on that join table as one of our custom features.
