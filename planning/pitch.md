

0:00–0:30 — Problem

Many people find it difficult to track their daily income and expenses in one place.

0:30–1:15 — Solution

Our app is a budget tracker that allows users to add, edit, delete, filter, and categorize transactions.

1:15–2:15 — Main Features

Dashboard
Quick transaction modal
Multiple categories per transaction
Filtering and sorting
Default categories
Validation and error messages
2:15–2:45 — Database Design

Transactions and categories have a many-to-many relationship through the Transaction_Categories join table.

2:45–3:00 — Closing

Our goal is to make personal expense tracking simple, organized, and easy to use.
